Telescope Control

Most telescope manufacturers have a line of telescopes with a computerized mount.
A computer-controlled mount can save a load of time for amateur and professional astronomers alike, however, there are power requirements to be considered.
This section assumes a home observatory or computer controlled telescope at home.
A computer controlled telescope works in tandem with CCD software.
Many planetarium software packages have an advanced or professional level software that are capable of controlling a telescope using ASCOM standards - an open source language used by Astronomers.
Telescope control software controls the telescope's electronic mount. The CCD software works in tandem with the telescope control software to:

  • Ensure accuracy by tracking a guide star.
  • Focus the eyepiece.
  • Capture the image.
